Potato

The main potato growing states in our country are Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal, Gujarat, Punjab, Assam, Madhya Pradesh. Uttar Pradesh ranks among potato growing states, where 35.5 percent of the total area of ​​potato in the country and 41.1 percent of the total production.

Field selection and preparation:-

For good potato production, flat and highly fertile, sandy loam and loamy soils with good drainage are best. Leave the field empty after 2-3 ploughings. In order to get a good crop of potatoes, it is necessary to do plava before sowing. In case of injury, plow as required and level the field by using a pat.

Fertilizers and Fertilizers::-

According to the results of the tests, generally 180 kg for good yield of potato horticulture require nitrogen, 80 kg phosphorus and 100 kg potash. if the soil If there is deficiency of micro elements like zinc and iron, then apply in the field before sowing with fertilizers at the rate of 25 kg zinc sulphate and 50 kg ferrous sulphate per hectare respectively.

Time of sowing::-

Early crop is usually sown around 15th September and dug in 70-80 days She goes. 15 October to 25 October is the appropriate time for sowing the main crop..

Seed Quantity:-

Disease free, pure seed of potato should always be procured from reliable sources, especially from government institutions, state seed corporations or seed production agencies. For sowing in the field, use good germinated seeds weighing about 40 to 50 grams. Normally 30 35 quintals (3540 grams) or (3.5 to 4.0 cm in size) tubers are sufficient..

Sowing Method:-

Keep the row distance 60 cm and the distance from the tuber to the tuber according to the size of the seed potato. can be adjusted. Seed tubers of size 20, 40, 60 and 80 grams are given 15 20. 30 and 40 cm. Potatoes are sown in sheep/gulls at a depth of 8-10 cm with the help of a scraper, so that sufficient moisture remains in the soil for germination.

Weed control and soil plating:-

20-25 days after potato sowing, plant 8-10 cm. If the height of the lines is Do weed removal with a mid-spring-tine cultivar or khurpi. Meanwhile, nitrogen Put the remaining quantity on the gullies and bury the soil with a shovel or tractor operated riser. In plain areas, the infestation of weeds is more in potato crop after 4-6 weeks of sowing. That is, by this method the crop should remain weed free. Irrigation:-

7 to 10 irrigations are required to get good yield. of sowing First irrigation before germination after 8-10 days and after 10-12 days in heavy soils. While irrigating, keep in mind that potato husks should not be more than two-thirds immersed in water, otherwise abiotic conditions arise in the soil due to excess water. Second irrigation in potato crop 20 22 days after sowing (at the time of branch-branches) and third irrigation immediately after trapping and earthing (in the early stage of tuber formation) i.e. about four weeks after sowing. Crop Protection::-

Disease Control:

Scorching disease : This disease affects all the leaves, stems and stems of the plant. early onset of the disease Symptoms appear as small, pale yellow, green irregularly shaped spots on the leaves, which They grow and form wet-looking spots, later on the lower part of the leaves, ring-like white mold comes around these spots.

Control:: –

resistant varieties like Kufri Satlut, Kufri Anand, Kufri Badshah, Kufri Dhipsona-1, Kufri Chipsona-2 and Kufri Sinduri. Sorting and separating diseased tubers before storage and sowing the crop in the pit Press it. When the weather is favorable for the disease, stop irrigation and 75 percent leaves are destroyed, but after cutting the stalks, bury them in the pit outside the field. Before showing signs of disease on the crop, make a solution of 02 percent (2 grams per liter of water) and spray it. After that keep spraying at an interval of 8-10 days. In severe outbreak of scorch, 12 sprays of 0.25 percent solution of drugs containing metalaxyl should be done..

Excavation:-

Excavation can be done after 60-70 days of sowing to get higher price from early crop. The main crop should be excavated before the temperature reaches 20 to 30 ° C, so that the crop does not suffer from diseases like wilt and black rot at high temperature.

Yield:-

Early maturing yields are relatively low, whereas long duration varieties give high yields. Higher yields from hybrid varieties than normal varieties (600 800 quintals per hectare).
